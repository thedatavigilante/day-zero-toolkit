# TikTok Hook Formulas — The Data Vigilante

These are proven hook patterns. Use as reference when prompting or reviewing output.

## Formula 1: The Buried Stat
> "Most people don't know that [specific number/stat] — and it's costing them [specific consequence]."

Example:
> "67% of laid-off federal workers don't file for UCFE. That's money left on the table — and you have 21 days."

## Formula 2: The Wrong Assumption
> "You've been told [common belief]. That's not how it works."

Example:
> "You've been told to wait 2 weeks before filing unemployment. That's wrong. File day one."

## Formula 3: The Hidden Right
> "If you [situation], you have the right to [specific thing most people don't know]."

Example:
> "If you were laid off with more than 20 coworkers, you have the right to 60 days advance notice — or 60 days pay."

## Formula 4: The Stacked Resource
> "[Program 1] + [Program 2] + [Program 3] can be stacked simultaneously. Here's how."

Example:
> "Unemployment insurance + SNAP + vocational rehab can run at the same time. Most people only claim one."

## Formula 5: The Deadline Drop
> "You have [X days/hours] from [event] to [action]. After that, you lose [specific thing]."

Example:
> "You have 60 days from your last day of coverage to elect COBRA. Miss it and you're uninsured with no reinstatement option."
