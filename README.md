## The Black Box Key

Most people in the DMV never learn this:

**DORS / RSA / DARS** (vocational rehabilitation) can be stacked on top of unemployment insurance and food assistance simultaneously — and it can pay for retraining, certifications, college, assistive technology, transportation, and even business startup costs.

Neurodivergent individuals, people with anxiety/depression/PTSD/ADHD, chronic illness — you may qualify. The bar is whether your condition creates a barrier to employment, not whether it's severe.

| State | Agency | Apply |
|-------|--------|-------|
| DC | RSA — DC Dept of Disability Services | [dds.dc.gov](https://dds.dc.gov/service/how-apply-rsa-services) · 202-442-8400 |
| Maryland | DORS — Division of Rehabilitation Services | [dors.maryland.gov](https://dors.maryland.gov) · 1-888-554-0334 |
| Virginia | DARS — Dept for Aging and Rehabilitative Services | [dars.virginia.gov](https://www.dars.virginia.gov/drs/vr/) · 1-800-552-5019 |
