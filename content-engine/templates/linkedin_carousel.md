# LinkedIn Carousel Structure — The Data Vigilante

## Standard 7-Slide Format

**Slide 1 — HOOK**
One bold statement. Large font. Dark background.
No paragraph. The whole slide is one sentence.
> Example: "Most federal workers don't know they have 3 unemployment programs available simultaneously."

**Slide 2 — THE PROBLEM**
What most people do (wrong) and why it costs them.
1–2 sentences. Specific.

**Slide 3 — INSIGHT 1**
First tactical piece of information.
Program name, eligibility, deadline, or dollar amount.

**Slide 4 — INSIGHT 2**
Second tactical piece. Different angle from slide 3.

**Slide 5 — INSIGHT 3**
Third tactical piece. If applicable, the "stacking" move — combining resources.

**Slide 6 — THE MOVE**
What to do right now. Numbered steps (3 max).
Specific actions, not vague advice.

**Slide 7 — CTA**
Where to go next.
- Link to Day Zero Toolkit
- Follow @neurospicydatawhiz on TikTok
- DM "TOOLKIT" for the resource

---

## Design Notes
- Each slide: max 40 words
- Use high contrast (dark bg / light text or vice versa)
- Slide 1 always gets the most visual weight
- No stock photos. Use data visualizations, screenshots, or plain text slides.
